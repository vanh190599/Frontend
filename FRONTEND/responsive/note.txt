- Cac cong nghe de thuc hien responsive:
	- Su dung HTML5
	- Nhan dien thiet bi truy cap de tro den website phu hop
- Lap trinh mobile
	- Web app
	- Hybrid app
		- Phonegap, cordova, ionic
	- Native app
- Mot so khai niem:
	- De thuc hien resposonsive, can dat the sau vao trong the head
		<meta name="viewport" content="width:device-width">
	- @media all -> tat ca cac thiet bi
	- @media screent -> thiet bi la man hinh
	- max-width -> do rong toi da
	- min-width -> do rong toi thieu
	- max-height -> do cao toi da
	- min-heig -> do cao toi thieu
